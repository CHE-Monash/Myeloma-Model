# DVd Calibrated Transport - cross-scenario results

Bootstrap mean with 95% percentile CI (500 iterations; A and B bootstrapped). MAE is vs the raw observed DVd distribution (n=533, fixed); C_mrdr is retained for the BCR table and economics. Generated by compare_scenarios.do.

## BCR prediction accuracy (DVd, vs observed C)
- MAE, Traditional (A): 8.5 pp (95% CI 6.2-10.4)
- MAE, Calibrated Transport (B): 4.3 pp (95% CI 1.6-7.9)
- Reduction: 4.1 pp; 48% (95% CI -3-81%)

## ICER, DVd vs Vd (discounted; point = ratio of mean inc cost / mean inc QALY)
- A Traditional: 459,235 (95% CI 375,908 to 625,665)
- B Calibrated Transport: 855,941 (95% CI 493,316 to 4,172,115)
- C Observed: 2,396,870 (95% CI -18989536 to 19142824)

Note: ICER percentile CIs can be unstable where the incremental QALY is near zero (notably scenario C); the inc-cost and inc-QALY CIs in icer_comparison.csv are more directly interpretable there.
